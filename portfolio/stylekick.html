Stylekick Inc.

I joined this fast-growing fashion tech startup in May 2014. After a 4 month internship, I was promoted to my current position as an SEO & Marketing Analyst.

I was initially brought in for SEO (search engine optimization) work, but I moved onto user-acquisition for the company app.

I focused on:

  • ASO (app store optimization)
  • SEO on the Stylekick Blog & Outfit Showcase pages
  • Mobile advertising
  • Innovative social-media campaigns

Facebook App-Install Ads

I targeted the United States (US), United Kingdom (UK) Canada, Australia, Germany, France & Sweden. The goal was to get the Stylekick's Facebook CPI to less than $1.00. According to Fiksu, the CPI (cost-per-install) of a loyal user as of October 2014 is $2.16 - see source.

I managed to get the CPI of Stylekick's iOS app approximately 69.2% more cost-efficent (not incl. Sweden & testing campaigns) than the index under Facebook's advertising platform.
